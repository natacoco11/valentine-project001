Letter to Stefanie

Will you be my Valentine, Stefanie?

I knew you would say yes! Happy Valentine's Day, Stefanie! I'm so lucky to have you in my life. I hope this day is as special as you are to me. You're my constant companion and the most important person in my life. You make every day brighter just by being you. You're compassionate, kind, and the most wonderful person I've ever met. I love you more than words can express.
